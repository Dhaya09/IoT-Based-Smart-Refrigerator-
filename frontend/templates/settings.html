🔧 SMTP Configuration

To enable email alerts, configure your SMTP credentials in backend/mail.py:

SMTP_HOST = "smtp.gmail.com"
SMTP_PORT = 587
SMTP_USER = "your_email@gmail.com"
SMTP_PASS = "your_app_password"
💡 Gmail Tip: Use an App Password (not your normal password). Enable 2FA on your Google account first.
📋 Alert Rules Reference
🚨 Critical Rules
  • 🧪 Ammonia (MQ-137) > 2500 ppm
  • 🔥 LPG > 3000 ppm
  • ☠️ CO > 200 ppm
  • 🚪 Door open > 30 seconds
⚠️ Warning Rules
  • 🌡️ Temperature > 8°C or < 1°C
  • 💧 Humidity > 80% or < 30%
  • 🌫️ Air Quality > 2500 ppm
  • ⚖️ Weight < 1000 g
  • 📊 Pressure < 1000 hPa
  • 🥛 Milk Level < 500 ml
  • 💡 Door open + Light > 70%
✅ Email Triggers
  • 📧 All Critical alerts → Email
  • 📧 All Warning alerts → Email
  • 📧 Subject: Smart Fridge Alert 🚨
  • 📧 Includes: sensor, value, action
  • 📧 HTML formatted email
